About Us

We Believe In Helping
Each Other

Charitize is a global nonprofit organization dedicated to creating lasting change in underserved communities. Since 2004, we've been partnering with local leaders to address critical issues: education, clean water, healthcare, and economic opportunity.

Our approach centers on community-led solutions, long-term partnerships, and transparent accountability to every donor and volunteer who makes our work possible.

  • Community-driven programs with measurable impact
  • 100% of donations directed to active programs
  • Partnerships with 200+ local NGOs worldwide
  • Award-winning transparency and accountability

Our Mission

Empowering Communities
Through Action

We believe that poverty, inequality, and suffering are not inevitable — they are the result of broken systems that can be fixed with collective action, compassion, and smart investment.

Our mission is to mobilize resources, build local capacity, and amplify the voices of those who are most often left behind. Together, we are writing a different story.
